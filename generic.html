Technology

Analytical Chemistry Techniques
that are
User Friendly and Easy to Use!

Wastewater technicians are not chemists and nor should they be expected to know how to analyze for trace chemicals in wastewater. At WDPD2WWTP we provide an easy to technology that extracts and analyzes a group of compounds of interest. Most preparatory work to extract trace compounds from high matrix samples take a lot of time, but here WDPD2WWTP we believe your time is valueable!

Our technology automates solid-phase extraction sample preparation and liquid chromatography triple quarupole mass spectrometer (LC-QqQ-MS) analysis. With each group of pharmaceutical compounds of interest a premade reagent kit is loaded and a preset software is selected to target specific analytes. Since each class of compounds runs in either positive or negative mode it is necessary to analyze them under different conditions. That is why our instrument and analysis software is preset to provide the best data report possible. The QqQ system utilizes an electrospray ionization source to mitiage any premature fragmentation before analysis while keeping limits of detection low. The sample analysis of the QqQ runs in selected ion mode to pick out our compounds of interest and then fragment them to verify their identity based on a curated library. Additionally, isotope labeled internal standards are injected into each sample before analysis to quantify the amound of loss and interference from matrix suppression.
